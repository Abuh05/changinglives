“We are dedicated to combating the pervasive issue of substance abuse in Nigeria.”

At the Foundation for Changing Lives Against Substance Abuse,
we recognize the urgent need to address the high prevalence of drug use in Nigeria.
Our Mission
To enlighten the public on the dangers of substance abuse as well as to treat, rehabilitate and facilitate the recovery of those addicted to drugs.
Our Vision
To have a country where the citizens are safe, healthy and drug-free.
More About Us

Our Service

Our foundation offers a range of services aimed at
preventing substance abuse and supporting individuals on the path to recovery. These services include

Evidence-based Drug Prevention Training Programs

Designed to educate individuals on the dangers of drug and alcohol abuse.

Drug Kit Exhibition for Drug Education Services

Providing support and guidance for those struggling with addiction.

School Based prevention outreach

Engaging with schools, faith-based organizations, and community leaders to raise awareness and promote prevention efforts.

Drug treatment and rehabilitation services

Offering guidance and encouragement for individuals and families affected by substance abuse.
