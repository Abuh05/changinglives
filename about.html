Be part of a change
you want to see in the world

“We are dedicated to combating the pervasive issue of substance abuse in Nigeria.”

At the Foundation for Changing Lives Against Substance Abuse,
we recognize the urgent need to address the high prevalence of drug use in Nigeria.
Our Mission
To enlighten the public on the dangers of substance abuse as well as to treat, rehabilitate and facilitate the recovery of those addicted to drugs.
Our Vision
To have a country where the citizens are safe, healthy and drug-free.
